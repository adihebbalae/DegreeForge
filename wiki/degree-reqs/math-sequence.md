# Math Sequence & Requirements

Covers the math prerequisite chain for BSE ECE + the optional Math BA double major that Adithya is considering.

---

## ECE Math Prerequisite Chain

```
M 408C (Diff & Integral Calc, 4 hr)
    └──→ M 408D (Integral Calc, Sequences & Series, 4 hr)   ← needed for Honors ECE prereqs
    └──→ M 408S (alternative to 408D)
    └──→ ECE 402 / ECE 406 (coreq or prereq)
M 427J (Diff Eqs w/ Linear Algebra, 4 hr)  ← prereq: M 408C or 408D
    └──→ ECE 313 (Linear Systems)
    └──→ ECE 351K (Probability)
    └──→ M 427L (Adv Calc for Applications II) ← needed for all EE tech core tracks
M 340L / M 341 (Linear Algebra)  ← recommended; prereq for some upper-div math
M 325K (Discrete Math, 3 hr)  ← required for CE tech core tracks (Adithya's path)
```

## Key Observations

1. **M 325K double-dips**: It satisfies both the CE tech core advanced math requirement AND the Math BA "Introduction to Proofs" requirement.
2. **M 427J double-dips**: Satisfies ECE core prerequisite AND counts toward Math BA additional UD hours slot.
3. **M 427L is NOT needed**: Only required if switching to EE tech core track. Adds 4 hours of work if pursued for Math BA.
4. **Math BA feasibility**: Given ECE course load targets of 17-18 hrs/semester and summer willingness, Math BA adds ~3-4 extra courses beyond normal ECE path.
