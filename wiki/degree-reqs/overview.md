# BSE ECE Degree Requirements — Overview

UT Austin 2026-2028 catalog. Bachelor of Science in Engineering — Electrical and Computer Engineering (BSECE).

**~128 total credit hours** spread across 6 categories.

---

## Key Rules

- **Catalog year**: 2026-2028. Pre-2026 course numbers (ECE 302/306/312/319K) map to 2026 numbers (ECE 402/406/412/419K) — all are interchangeable in the planner.
- **Normalize**: `E E` → `ECE` at data load time. All internal references use `ECE`.
- **120 hr minimum** for graduation; most students land at 128-132.
- **Upper-division minimum**: 60 UD hours required for degree.
- **Tech Core**: 8 upper-division courses (7 for ECB dual degree). One declared track.
- **Honors variants**: ECE 402→ECE 302H, ECE 412→ECE 312H, ECE 419K→ECE 319H. Honors counts for the same core slots.
