# ECE Core Required Courses

10 courses required for all BSE ECE students regardless of tech core track. ~30 credit hours.

> **Catalog note**: 2026-2028 uses new course numbers. Old numbers (pre-2026) still appear in transcripts and are equivalent. Honors variants exist for ECE 402, ECE 412, and ECE 419K.

---

## Core Courses

| 2026 Number | Pre-2026 | Title | Credits | Offered | Key Prereqs |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| ECE 402 | ECE 302 | Introduction to Electrical Engineering | 5 | Fall + Spring | M 408C (or coreq) |
| ECE 406 | ECE 306 | Introduction to Computing | 3 | Fall + Spring | M 408C (or coreq) |
| ECE 412 | ECE 312 | Software Design & Implementation I | 3 | Fall + Spring | ECE 406 |
| ECE 419K | ECE 319K | Introduction to Embedded Systems | 3 | Fall + Spring | ECE 406 |
| ECE 411 | ECE 411 | Circuit Theory | 3 | Fall + Spring | ECE 402 |
| ECE 313 | ECE 313 | Linear Systems and Signals | 3 | Fall + Spring | ECE 402, M 427J |
| ECE 351K | ECE 351K | Probability & Engineering Applications | 3 | Fall + Spring | M 427J |
| ECE 333T | ECE 333T | Engineering Communication | 3 | Fall + Spring | varies |
| ECE 364D | ECE 364D | Senior Design Preparation | 2 | Fall + Spring | senior standing |
| ECE 464K | — | Senior Design (or 464G/H/R/S) | 3-4 | Fall + Spring | ECE 364D |

**Total ECE core credits**: ~30-32 hrs

---

## Honors Variants

| Standard | Honors | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| ECE 402 (ECE 302) | ECE 302H | Prereq M 408D instead of M 408C; more rigorous |
| ECE 412 (ECE 312) | ECE 312H | Same slot, honors version |
| ECE 419K (ECE 319K) | ECE 319H | Same slot, honors version |

Honors variants count for the same core requirement slot. No distinction in degree audit.
