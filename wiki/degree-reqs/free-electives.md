# Free Electives — Rules & Constraints

**14 total free elective hours** required for BSE ECE. Highly constrained — read all rules before planning.

---

## Rules

1. **At least 3 hours** must be approved advanced math or basic science
2. **No more than 3 hours** of lower-division credit
3. **AP credits never accepted** toward free electives (zero exceptions)
4. **No more than 3 hours** of transfer credit
5. **All coursework must count for a major** in the offering department (i.e., cross-listed courses must count for the dept offering it)
6. **All coursework must be taken in residence** — except up to 3 transfer hours
7. **No duplication**: No course can duplicate a course required elsewhere in the ECE degree

---

## Practical Implications

- Double-major courses (e.g., Math BA) can fill free elective slots if they don't duplicate other ECE requirements
- Summer courses (online or in-person) count toward the 3-hr transfer cap
- CTI certificate courses (CTI 301G, CTI 302, etc.) may count as free electives if they don't duplicate core curriculum requirements
- ECE 316 may count as Advanced Tech Elective if not part of declared tech core (Adi's Computer Arch track already includes ECE 316 as a core course, so it cannot also be the Advanced Tech Elective)

---

## Advanced Tech Elective (1 course, 3-4 hr)

Separate from free electives but worth noting here:
- 1 additional upper-division ECE course from **any** tech area
- ECE 316 may count **if not already part of declared tech core** (not applicable for Adi)
- Alternatives: 2 semesters of ECE Co-op OR 1 semester ECE 125S
