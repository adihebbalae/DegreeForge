# Tech Core Tracks — All 10 Tracks

Each student declares ONE tech core. 8 upper-division courses (7 for ECB dual degree) within the declared track. Structure: 1 advanced math + 2 core courses + 1 core lab + 3-4 tech electives.

> **Adithya's declared track**: Computer Architecture & Embedded Systems — CE category. See [[tech-cores/computer-arch-embedded]] for full detail.

## EE tracks (require M 427L)

All EE tracks require **M 427L** (Advanced Calculus for Applications II) as the advanced math component. Most require **ECE 325** (Electromagnetic Engineering).

---

## CE tracks (require M 325K)

All CE tracks require **M 325K** (Discrete Mathematics) as the advanced math component. Adithya is in a CE track and is currently taking M 325K (Spring 2026). ✓
