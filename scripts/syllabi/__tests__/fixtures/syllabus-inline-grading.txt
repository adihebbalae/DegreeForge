EE 411 Circuit Theory — Spring 2024

Catalogue Data: EE 411. Circuit Theory. Capacitance and inductance; first- and second-order
transient circuit response; sinusoidal steady-state analysis using phasors.

Textbook: C. K. Alexander and M. N. O. Sadiku, Fundamentals of Electric Circuits, 7th
edition, New York: McGraw-Hill, 2021.

Grade Weights: Examination 1: 20%, Examination 2: 25%, Final Examination: 35%,
Homework: 10%, and Labs: 10%. The numerical course grade is computed by weighting
the raw scores as indicated above.
